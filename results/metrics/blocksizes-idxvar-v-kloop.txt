=== LAPLAP-IDXVAR VS LAPLAP-KLOOP AT 128 (KLOOP OPTIMAL) AND 512 THREADS (IDXVAR OPTIMAL) ===
Why does performance decrease with more threads for kloop whereas it increases for more threads for idxvar?
 -> Too low occupancy in kloop variants!
